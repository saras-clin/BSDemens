  Problem:  Persons who emigrate from Denmark leave the Danish registers and

            unobservable. Not censoring at emigration overstates person-time and
            can bias incidence rate estimates.
  Current:  Persons are only censored at death (dod register) or 2025-12-31.
            Emigration is not a censoring event in any script.
  Reference: archive/other peoples code/healthcarecosts2007_2020.R uses a pre-computed
            "LifeLines1986_2021.RData" dataset (project 708614) with entry_d/exit_d columns
            tracking when each person is registered in Denmark. The underlying data comes
            from bef_bop (bop_vtil = residence-until date) and bef_adr registers.
  Action:   Ask data manager: what is the register/variable for exact emigration
            date on project 708421?
            Likely candidates:
              - vnds (vandrestatistik / migration statistics) register
              - bef_bop register with bop_vtil (residence-until date) — confirmed approach
                in reference project 708614 (see healthcarecosts2007_2020.R)
              - CPR direct extract with emigration date
            Approximate alternative: detect last year of BEF appearance and use
            January 1 of the following year as a proxy emigration date (loses
            precision but captures most cases).
  Code:     Add emigration date to extract_demographics() in

Official name: Databasen for Behandling af Svær Overvægt (DBSO)

Researcher access: https://forskningsadgang.sundk.dk/
Documentation portal: https://www.db-dokumentation-sundk.dk/Public/Databases.aspx?db=34

Variables confirmed to be in DBSO:
  - Surgery date, surgery type (SKS codes KJDF10/11/40/41/96/97)
  - Weight (kg) and height (cm) at: pre-exam, 1-year follow-up, 2-year follow-up
  - BMI calculated from weight/height
  - Diabetes medications (insulin, oral agents) at baseline and follow-up
  - Lipid-lowering, antihypertensive medications
  - Acute readmissions and reoperations within 30 days

Note: 5-year complication data in DBSO annual reports is derived from LPR,
not from the DBSO registry module itself.
