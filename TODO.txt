  Impact:   Minor for NMI baseline characterisation in Study 1. More relevant for
            Study 2 (T1D classification) where diabetes type precision matters more.
            The OSDC pre-computed file already handles this for primary classification.
  Action:   Add indo to the lmdb select() in extract_baseline_medications().
            Exclude PCOS metformin: filter out atc == "A10BA02" AND sex == 2 AND
            indo %in% c("0000092", "0000276", "0000781") before flagging antidiabetic.
  Code:     extract_outcomes_covariates.R — extract_baseline_medications()

Official name: Databasen for Behandling af Svær Overvægt (DBSO)
Operated by:   SunDK (formerly RKKP)
Coverage:      All public and private hospitals; mandatory since 2010

Researcher access: https://forskningsadgang.sundk.dk/
Documentation portal: https://www.db-dokumentation-sundk.dk/Public/Databases.aspx?db=34

Variables confirmed to be in DBSO:
  - Surgery date, surgery type (SKS codes KJDF10/11/40/41/96/97)
  - Weight (kg) and height (cm) at: pre-exam, 1-year follow-up, 2-year follow-up
  - BMI calculated from weight/height
  - Diabetes medications (insulin, oral agents) at baseline and follow-up
  - Lipid-lowering, antihypertensive medications
  - Acute readmissions and reoperations within 30 days

Note: 5-year complication data in DBSO annual reports is derived from LPR,
not from the DBSO registry module itself.
